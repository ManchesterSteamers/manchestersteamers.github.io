About

About the team.

The Manchester Steamers are a planned recreational team whose aim is to provide an inclusive, friendly space for Manchester Metros Players, Metro Alumni and members of the wider Metros Community to enjoy and to continue playing ice hockey after their studies.

The aim is currently to operate over the off season, through 10 weeks of scrimmages from June to September, in order to establish and set the foundations of developing the team.​​

We are currently looking to gauge interest to begin the team, if you are interested, please complete the Google Form.

Eligibility

Find out the requirements for joining us.

You must be an adult to play for us.

You must have some previous affiliation with the Manchester Metros. This could be a current Metros player, Former Metros player or Alumni, or a friend of the Metros community. We can be somewhat flexible with this, so feel free to try!
